Tangy Burger sauce

This is a tangy burger sauce for the American Burgers. It also goes great with fries.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/4 cup yellow mustard
  • 2 teaspoons of dried onion, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoons of dried garlic, minced
  • 1/8 teaspoon white vinegar
  • hot sauce to taste
  • seasoned pepper to taste
  1. Whisk together the mayo, ketchup, mustard, onion, garlic and vinegar in a bowl. Season it with hot sauce and black pepper to taste.
  2. Cover and chill for at least 1 hour before serving.
