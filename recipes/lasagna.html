Odin's Best Lasagna

This lasagna should take about an hour and a half total. Features customizable layers.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Egg
  • Cheeses
  • Lasagna noodles

Step-by-step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna. (Noodles, Meat sauce, cheese mix, meat sauce, cheese mix, repeat.)
  5. Cover with foil and bake. (50 mins at 375F + 25 mins uncovered)
  6. Let lasagna rest for 10-15 mins before serving.
