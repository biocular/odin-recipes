American-style burger (basic)

This burger takes nearly no time to make (20 mins total) and serves four. It's simple and requires common ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Step-by-step

  1. Prepare grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Whisk together egg, salt and pepper.
  3. Mix ground beef and bread crumbs with previous mixture. Use fork if needed, mix well.
  4. Form into four 3/4-inch thick patties.
  5. Place patties onto grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. Thermometer into center shoudl read at least 160F.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy. Add our tangy burger sauce for some extra heat.
